Study Shakespeare with our simple but challenging quiz app

Study the bard on the go - act by act. Choose from twenty plays. Then test your knowledge with one of our nearly 1,200 quizzes. Plus dozens of study tips!

Easy-to-use

Choose a play. Study an act. Take a quiz. Follow the study tips. There are 20 plays to choose from including Hamlet, King Lear, Macbeth, The Merchant of Venice, A Midsummer Night’s Dream, Much Ado About Nothing, Othello, Romeo and Juliet and The Tempest.

Self-paced

By studying Shakespeare act by act, you can get to know a play’s characters and their actions and quotes at a pace that’s right for you. Build your confidence by mastering an act and take that confidence with you into the rest of the play.

Multi-level

Choose your level of difficulty: easy, medium or hard. Easy quizzes test your knowledge of characters, scenes and quotes, as well as the meanings of Shakespearean words. Medium quizzes test you on Shakespeare’s descriptive language. Hard quizzes focus on longer quotes and more obscure facts from the play.
